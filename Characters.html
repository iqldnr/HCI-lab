Characters

Irvan

Irvan is a bartender who survived this world disaster. Irvan is called by people as a lucky bartender because Irvan has a high level of luck so he is adored by many people.

Izra

Izra was formerly an activist in community organizations who often carried out joint activities, he was one of the survivors of the destruction of the world. Izra is an expert in using knives and guns.

Danta

Danta is a former prisoner at the notorious Alcatraz prison. Danta is proficient in using a long sword to fight.
